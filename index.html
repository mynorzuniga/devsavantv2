Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with startups at any stage?›

Yes. We support from early-stage founders without a tech team or revenue to established companies +300M in ARR that need to accelerate initiatives or extend technical capabilities.

How do you validate whether my idea is technically feasible?›

We run a fast Discovery process to validate assumptions, technical feasibility, and risks. You receive clear insights, budget estimates, and an execution blueprint before any development begins.

How quickly can you deliver a prototype or MVP?›

Depending on scope, we deliver prototypes within weeks and MVPs in short iterations, supported by our AI-first approach and a team that scales dynamically.

How do you integrate AI into what you build?›

We identify where AI creates real value—automation, copilots, workflows, custom models, LLM integrations, or enhancements to existing processes—and apply it intentionally.

How transparent is the process in terms of cost, timeline, and roadmap?›

You receive upfront estimates, a clear plan, and ongoing visibility. No surprises, just aligned expectations and consistent communication.

What happens after the MVP is delivered? Do you offer post-launch support?›

Yes. We can continue iterating, expand features, integrate AI, or transition the product to your internal team. You can also move into staff augmentation, where a dedicated engineer fully embeds into your team for post-delivery needs and long-term stability.

How do you source and vet talent in LATAM and Asia?›

We run a rigorous process including our proprietary Startup Profile interview, cultural fit evaluations, and technical assessments. Only top-tier talent moves forward.

Can you build a team quickly if we need to scale fast?›

Yes. Our recruiting pipelines in LATAM and Asia allow us to present the first candidates within 2 weeks, ensuring speed without compromising quality.

How do you ensure long-term stability and low turnover?›

Our engineers are fully embedded into your team, and all day-to-day management is handled directly by you. We stay in a consultative role providing market insights and compensation guidance. This shared model keeps talent aligned, motivated, and consistent over time.

Can your staff integrate with our internal team and processes?›

Absolutely. Our team members embed fully into your workflows, tools, standups, and delivery rhythm, functioning as an extension of your team.

Do you offer flexible engagement models?›

Yes. You can scale up or down as needs evolve, with transparent costs and flexible terms.

Depending on scope, we deliver prototypes within weeks and MVPs in short iterations. We can integrate with what you already have or recommend a new architecture if it’s more efficient or cost-effective.
