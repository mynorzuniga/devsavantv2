Savant Growth, a Success Story

Savant Growth is a US-based growth equity fund known for scaling capital-efficient B2B tech companies into category leaders. Over three decades, its partners have invested nearly $1B across the US and Europe, driving consistent growth outcomes. A core part of this success has been their partnership with DevSavant, acting as their dedicated operating partner. Our team works directly with CEOs, CTOs, and CFOs across their portfolio to streamline operations, enhance product execution, and implement projects that accelerate growth.

Together with Kennet Partners, Savant Growth leverages this operational and technical strength to run specialized investment programs that push companies toward global leadership. Their investment philosophy focuses on businesses using software, data, analytics, and AI to transform workflows and unlock the next wave of digital efficiency.
